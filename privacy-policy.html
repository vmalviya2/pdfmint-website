Privacy Policy for PDFMint

Last updated 27 June 2026

Last updated: 27 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PDFMint ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use our Android application. PDFMint is developed by VoxelNest Studio (Vivek Malviya) ("the developer").

By using PDFMint, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Summary (the short version)

  • PDFMint converts images to PDF and lets you scan, merge, and compress PDFs.
  • All document processing happens on your device. Your images and PDFs are not uploaded to our servers — we don't have any servers that receive them.
  • We do not require an account, name, email, or login to use the app.
  • We show ads through Google AdMob, which may collect device and usage data (including an advertising identifier) to serve and measure ads.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide / files you choose

When you use PDFMint you may select photos, images, or PDF files and use the camera to scan documents. These files are processed locally on your device to create or modify PDFs. The resulting PDFs are stored only on your device (in the app's storage) and appear in your in-app History. We do not access, transmit, or store these files on any server.

Information collected automatically by third parties

PDFMint uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob and the Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and process information such as:

  • a device advertising identifier (Advertising ID),
  • approximate, non-precise location derived from IP address,
  • device information (model, operating system, language),
  • ad interaction and performance data.

This collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy and Google's advertising practices: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

We ourselves do not collect, see, or store this advertising data — it is handled by Google.

2. Permissions we use and why

  • Camera — to scan documents directly within the app. Images are processed on-device only.
  • Photos / Media (storage) — to let you pick images and save/open PDFs on your device.
  • Internet / Network state — required to load advertisements.

You can manage or revoke these permissions at any time in your device's Settings. Denying camera or photo access will limit related features but you can still use the rest of the app.

3. How information is used

  • To provide the app's core features (create, scan, merge, compress PDFs).
  • To display and measure advertisements via Google AdMob.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data sharing

We do not share your files or personal information with third parties. The only data sharing that occurs is the automated collection by Google AdMob for advertising, as described above.

5. Data retention and deletion

  • PDFs and app data are stored locally on your device. You can delete them at any time using Settings → Clear History, Settings → Clear Cache, or by deleting individual files in the History screen.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all app data stored on your device.
  • To reset your advertising identifier, use your device's Settings → Privacy → Ads controls.

6. Children's privacy

PDFMint is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can address it.

7. Security

Because your files stay on your device, their security depends on your device's own protections (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We encourage you to keep your device secure and up to date.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
