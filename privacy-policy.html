Privacy Policy for PDFMint

Last updated 27 June 2026

Last updated: 27 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PDFMint — Image to PDF ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use our Android application. PDFMint is developed by VoxelNest Studio (Vivek Malviya) ("the developer").

PDFMint is built around a privacy-first, local-first architecture: your photos, images, and PDF documents are processed entirely on your device and never leave it. By using PDFMint, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Summary (the short version)

  • PDFMint converts images to PDF and lets you scan, merge, and compress PDFs.
  • All document processing happens entirely on your device. Your photos and PDFs are never uploaded to us — we do not operate any document-processing servers that could receive them.
  • The only data collected is a device or other identifier, gathered automatically by Google AdMob to display and measure advertisements. This is collected directly by Google.
  • We do not require an account, name, email, or login to use the app, and we do not collect your photos, files, documents, location, or personal information.

1. Information we collect

Data we collect

The only category of data collected in connection with PDFMint is:

  • Device or other IDs — collected automatically by Google AdMob for advertising purposes (see Section 5).

This identifier is collected directly by Google to display and measure advertisements. We do not collect any other user data.

Data we do not collect

PDFMint does not collect, upload, or transmit any of the following. These stay on your device:

  • Photos, videos, images, files, or PDF documents
  • Contacts, calendar, or messages
  • Audio recordings
  • Personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or postal address
  • Precise or device-based location
  • App activity or browsing history

When you use PDFMint you may select photos, images, or PDF files and use the camera to scan documents. These files are read and processed locally on your device to create or modify PDFs. The resulting PDFs are stored only on your device (in the app's storage) and appear in your in-app History. We never access, transmit, upload, or store these files on any server.

2. On-device document processing

PDFMint is designed with privacy by design. Every document operation is performed entirely on your device:

  • Image to PDF conversion is performed entirely on-device.
  • PDF merge is performed entirely on-device.
  • PDF compression is performed entirely on-device.
  • Document scanning is performed entirely on-device.
  • OCR (text recognition), where available, is performed locally on-device.

As a result:

  • Your photos never leave your device.
  • Your PDF files never leave your device.
  • Your documents are never uploaded to our servers.
  • We do not operate any document-processing servers and perform no cloud-based document processing.

3. Permissions we use and why

  • Camera — to scan documents directly within the app. Captured images are processed on-device only.
  • Photos / Media (storage) — to let you select images, read PDFs, and save PDFs on your device.
  • Internet / Network state — used to load advertisements only.

Granting these permissions does not mean your documents are uploaded — they are used solely to enable on-device features and to load ads. You can manage or revoke permissions at any time in your device's Settings. Denying camera or photo access will limit related features, but you can still use the rest of the app.

4. How information is used

  • To provide the app's core features (convert, scan, merge, and compress PDFs) — all performed on your device.
  • To display and measure advertisements via Google AdMob, using a device identifier collected by Google.

We do not sell your personal information.

5. Google AdMob (advertising)

PDFMint uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. The Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and process information such as:

  • the Advertising ID and other device identifiers,
  • approximate, non-precise location inferred from your IP address,
  • device information (such as model, operating system, and language),
  • ad interaction and performance information.

This information is collected directly by Google and is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy and Google's advertising practices: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

We do not share your photos, PDF documents, or other files with third parties. Google AdMob may automatically collect certain device identifiers and advertising-related information in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy to display and measure advertisements. We ourselves do not receive your photos or PDF documents.

6. Data sharing

The documents you create or work with in PDFMint are:

  • never uploaded,
  • never shared,
  • never sold,
  • never transmitted off your device.

The only data handled outside your device is the advertising-related identifier that may be collected by Google AdMob in accordance with Google's own privacy practices, as described in Section 5. We do not share your files or personal information with any third party.

7. Data retention and deletion

  • Your documents, in-app History, and cache remain only on your device. No copies of your documents exist on our servers.
  • You can delete them at any time using Settings → Clear History, Settings → Clear Cache, or by deleting individual files in the History screen.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local application data stored on your device.
  • To reset your advertising identifier, use your device's Settings → Privacy → Ads controls.

8. Children's privacy

PDFMint is a general productivity application intended for general audiences. It is not specifically designed for or directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can address it.

9. Security

PDFMint follows Privacy by Design principles: offline, on-device processing and a local-first architecture with no cloud document processing. Because your files stay on your device, their security also depends on your device's own protections (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We encourage you to keep your device secure and up to date.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
