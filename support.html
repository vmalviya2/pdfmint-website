We typically respond within 2–3 business days.

When reporting a problem, please include:

  • your device model and Android version,
  • the PDFMint app version (see Settings → Version),
  • a short description of what happened and the steps to reproduce it,
  • a screenshot if possible.

Before you write — quick fixes

  • A feature isn't working? Make sure PDFMint has the required permission in your device Settings → Apps → PDFMint → Permissions.
  • Running low on space? Use Settings → Clear Cache and Settings → Clear History.
  • Looking for a how-to? Check the FAQ.

Feedback & feature requests

Have an idea to make PDFMint better? Use Settings → Send Feedback in the app, or email us directly. We read everything.
