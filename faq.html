Tap Scan Document on the home screen, capture the page, then crop, enhance, and save it as a PDF.

Open Merge PDF, tap the PDFs in the order you want them combined, then tap Merge PDFs.

Open Compress PDF, choose a PDF and a compression level (Fast, Balanced, or Maximum), then tap Compress PDF.

PDFMint's compression is designed to keep your documents readable. Higher compression ("Maximum") produces the smallest file; "Balanced" is recommended for most documents.

PDFMint works with standard photo formats such as JPG and PNG, whether you pick them from your gallery or capture them with your camera.
